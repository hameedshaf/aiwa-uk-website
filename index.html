About Our Association

Ar-Rahman Islamic Welfare Association is dedicated to providing support and guidance to the Tamil speaking Muslim community in Birmingham, UK.

Our mission is to enhance the well-being of the community through various initiatives such as Islamic education, health awareness, youth development, and women empowerment.

Join us in our journey to make a positive impact in the lives of those we serve.
